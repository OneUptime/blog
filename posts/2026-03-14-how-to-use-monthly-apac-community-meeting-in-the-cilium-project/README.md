## Joining the Monthly APAC Community Meeting

### Meeting Details

The Cilium APAC community meeting is held monthly, typically on the first Thursday, at a time convenient for Asia-Pacific timezones.

Find meeting details at:
- **Cilium Community Calendar**: Times are in UTC, convert to your local timezone
- **Slack #community-apac channel**: Meeting-specific discussions and reminders
- **GitHub cilium/community**: Schedule and agenda links

### Why an APAC Meeting Exists

The APAC meeting was created to ensure community members in Asia-Pacific timezones can participate in community discussions without attending meetings at inconvenient hours. Topics often focus on:

- Regional deployment patterns and challenges
- Features relevant to APAC infrastructure
- Community building in the APAC region
- General project updates

### Participating

- Check the agenda and add topics relevant to APAC users
- Share regional use cases and deployment experiences
- Connect with other APAC-based Cilium users and contributors
- Bring questions that benefit from timezone-appropriate discussion

## Verification

Verify meeting links work, agendas are accessible, and recordings are available.

## Troubleshooting

- **Cannot find meeting links**: Check the Cilium community calendar and #community Slack channel.
- **Slack workspace access**: Request an invite through the Cilium website.
- **GitHub permissions**: Ensure your account has the necessary access for the repositories you need.
- **Timezone confusion**: All official times are in UTC. Use a timezone converter for your local time.

## Conclusion

Community meetings provide opportunities to engaging with the Cilium community. Active participation strengthens both your own Cilium practice and the broader community.
